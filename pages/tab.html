Tableau Analysis

The data was modeled and presented using a tableau workbook. The following visualizations confirm the weak, but present correlation between home value and population. And presents the top cities and their corresponding days on market and home value.




Top 10 Cities and Days on Market








The visual depicts the regions with the highest days on market. It should be taken into consideration that amnogst the top ten cities, six of them are in the state of New York. Not shown here, the least number of days on market being primarily from the state of Washington in cities such as Olympia, Bremerton, Mount Vernon, and Bellingham.

Top 10 Cities and Home Values








The visual depicts the cities with the highest home values. Amongst the top ten cities with the highest home values, eight of the cities are in the state of California. Not pictured here, the state of Oklahoma has some cities ranking with the lowest home values in Ponca City, Muskogee, and McAlester. These cities in Oklahoma all have populations of less than 37,000.
